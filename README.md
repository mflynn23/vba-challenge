# vba-challenge

In this Week 2 homework assignment a script was created in VBA to run a stock analysis. The analysis captures a summary from each ticker symbol. The summary includes: Yearly change from the opening price at the beignning of the year to closing price of that year. Percentage change from the opening price at teh beginning of a given year to the closing price at the end of the year. The total stock volume of teh stock for that year.

The sciprt ran to get the results is included as well as a screenshot for each year after the script has been run.
